Meet the family

Satori has been part of Linden's DNA for over a decade. What started as one family's love for proper Italian food became the neighbourhood's living room — the place where birthdays get celebrated, deals get done over pasta, and Sunday afternoons slow down the way they're supposed to.

Owner Lloyd Newton runs the restaurant the old-fashioned way: fresh ingredients, recipes that don't cut corners, and a team that actually knows your name. That dedication earned Satori a Best Neighbourhood Restaurant award and a loyal following of 700+ Google reviews averaging 4.4 stars — not because of marketing, but because the food speaks for itself.

Everything on our menu is made in-house. We are not a chain. We are your restaurant.
